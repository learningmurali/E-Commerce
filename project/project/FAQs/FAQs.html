FAQs

1. Can I Return An Item ? +

Yes! We accept returns for any reason within 30 days of your order.Just send us an email with your Order Number and we'll send you a return label.

2. How Do I Change Or Cancel My Order ? +

We know how important your order is so we aim to fulfill orders as quickly as possible!While we cannot cancel or change an order once it has been placed.You are welcome to return your items in accordance with our Easy Returns Policy once your order has been delivered to you.

3. Do You Have a Click And Collect Service ? +

We currently cannot offer our chilled items for Click and Collect,but you are welcome to purchase these items seperately in our stores.

4. Do You Ship Internationally ? +

Due to the fresh nature of our products,we currently only ship within the UK,We also provide delivery services to Northern Ireland,Scottish Islands,scottish Highlands,Channel Isles and Isles of Wight.

Read more about our delivery services.

5. When Will My Order Ship ? +

After your payment is verified,it takes up to 24 hours to process and ship your order.This does not include weekends or holidays.Purchases made after 9 am PST will not be shipped out until the next business day.If you order after 9 am PST on a Friday,your order will likely be shipped out on the following Monday.
